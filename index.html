1968

Intel Founded

Robert Noyce and Gordon Moore found Intel, laying the foundation for decades of technological innovation.

1971

First Microprocessor

Intel releases the 4004, the world's first commercial microprocessor, revolutionizing computing.

1978

8086 Processor

The 8086 processor establishes the x86 architecture still used today.

1985

386 Processor

Intel introduces the 386 processor with 32-bit architecture, enabling multitasking.
